# 🏹 Arrow Strike - Archery Target Game

Test your archery skills in this addictive target shooting game! Aim your bow, charge your power, and hit bouncing balls to rack up combo multipliers and high scores!

## 🌟 Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Dynamic Bow Aiming** - Mouse/touch aim system with visual feedback
- **Power Charge Mechanic** - Hold to charge shot power (0-100%)
- **Bouncing Targets** - Physics-based balls that bounce around the screen
- **Realistic Projectile Physics** - Arrows affected by gravity and trajectory
- **Limited Arrows** - Strategic gameplay with 10 arrows per game
- **High Score Tracking** - Compete against your best accuracy

### Scoring System
- **Base Points** - Each target hit awards 100 points
- **Combo Multiplier** - Build up to 10x multiplier with consecutive hits
- **Miss Penalty** - Missing resets your combo multiplier
- **Accuracy Stats** - Track your hit percentage
- **Progressive Challenge** - Up to 8 targets bouncing simultaneously

### Visual Effects
- **Archery Aesthetic** - Red and purple gradient theme
- **Particle Explosions** - 30 particles burst on every hit
- **Score Popups** - Visual feedback showing points earned
- **Crosshair Cursor** - Professional aiming reticle
- **Power Meter** - Real-time visual charge indicator
- **Responsive Design** - Optimized for mobile and desktop

## 🎮 Controls

### Desktop
- **Mouse Movement** - Aim your bow
- **Click and Hold** - Charge power (watch the meter)
- **Release** - Shoot arrow

### Mobile
- **Touch and Drag** - Aim your bow
- **Touch and Hold** - Charge power
- **Release** - Shoot arrow

## 🎯 Gameplay Tips

1. **Charge Your Shots** - Higher power = faster arrows that reach farther targets
2. **Predict Movement** - Aim where the target will be, not where it is
3. **Build Combos** - Don't miss! Combos multiply your score exponentially
4. **Watch Gravity** - Arrows drop over distance, aim slightly higher
5. **Target Priority** - Hit closer targets first to build combo safely
6. **Limited Arrows** - You only get 10 shots, make each one count!

Simply open [index.html](index.html) in any modern web browser:

```bash

python3 -m http.server 8000

# Using Node.js
npx serve

```

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start Game** - Click "Start Game" on the welcome screen
2. **Aim** - Move your mouse/finger to aim the bow
3. **Charge** - Click and hold to charge power (watch the power meter)
4. **Shoot** - Release to fire the arrow
5. **Hit Targets** - Pop the bouncing balls for points
6. **Build Combos** - Hit consecutively to multiply your score
7. **Beat High Score** - Try to achieve 100% accuracy!

## 🏆 Scoring Breakdown

- **Hit Target**: 100 points × combo multiplier
- **Combo 1x**: First hit after game start or miss
- **Combo 2x**: Two consecutive hits (200 points per hit)
- **Combo 3x**: Three consecutive hits (300 points per hit)
- **...up to 10x**: Ten+ consecutive hits (1000 points per hit!)
- **Miss**: Resets combo back to 1x

## 💡 Advanced Strategies

- **Lead Your Shots**: Predict where fast-moving targets will be
- **Master the Arc**: Learn arrow trajectory for different power levels
- **Combo Protection**: Take easier shots when you have a high combo
- **Power Control**: Not every shot needs full power
- **Target Selection**: Hit the target that gives you the best angle
